# Product Vision

Transit Stats aims to eliminate the friction of transit tracking by automating trip logging, modeling personal ridership habits, and surfacing intelligence that riders couldn't generate manually.

## The North Star

- **Zero-Touch Logging**: Trips are detected and recorded passively — no SMS, no check-in, no manual input required.
- **Predictive Awareness**: The system knows your patterns well enough to suggest your next trip before you ask, and flag when your usual route is behaving abnormally.
- **Personal Transit Intelligence**: A long-term record of every trip, organized into insights that reflect how you actually use transit — not just raw logs.
